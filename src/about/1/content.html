SK Putrajaya Precinct 9 (1), (formerly known as SK Putrajaya 2) is one of the earliest schools in Putrajaya and one of the Smart Schools in Malaysia and was recognized as a Cluster School in 2009. Construction of the school began in mid -1998 and was fully completed for use on 17th January 2002, and was known as Sekolah Kebangsaan Putrajaya 2. The honor as the first Headmaster was given to Mr. Sukimin Bin Juki (my father) who took office on 24 December 1999.
