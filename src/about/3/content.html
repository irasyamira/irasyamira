Sekolah Seri Puteri (SSP) is a premier all-girlsboarding school (Sekolah Berasrama Penuh) located in Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia. In 2010, Sekolah Seri Puteri was awarded the prestigious Sekolah Berprestasi Tinggi or High Performance School title. The school specialises in wind orchestra and basketball as well as their debate team.
