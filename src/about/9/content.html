Hi! I am Ira Syamira Sukimin. Most of you may know me as "Ira" (pronounced as ee-rah) but if we go way back to primary school, I am most likely "Syam" to you (which is also the name my family calls me).

I have pretty much lived most of my life away from my family. I went straight to boarding school at the age of 13. Entered college right after, and then flew to New Zealand for my undergraduate studies. Soon after I returned from New Zealand, Alhamdulillah, I landed my current job at Intel Penang which forces me to again, live far away from my family. Nevertheless, they have been endlessly supportive of me and my decisions. Whatever I pursued, they cheered on for me.

I am a highly-introverted person so naturally I am a homebody. I don't mind being social once in a while when the situation demands for it, but of course at the end of every day all I want to do is just sit quietly by myself and unwind. I do push myself to step out of my comfort-zone once in a while and mingle with people to pursue my interests and grow as a person both in my personal and professional life.

I am a firm believer that there is something to learn from everything and everyone in the world if we have the right mindset, but in order to do so one must first leave the house :D
